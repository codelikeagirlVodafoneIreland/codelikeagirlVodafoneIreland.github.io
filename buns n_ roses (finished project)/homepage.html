about

Buns N' Roses is an Irish bakery located in South Dublin. We deliver within the province Leinster. Our delicacies are created by the finest quality bakers and made from scratch with fresh, organic ingredients. These marvellous goods have a fun twist to them as they are named after famous artists and songs. We are happy to customise the creations to suit any allergies or needs so you can indulge in the delicious taste without any concerns or worries. Just click on the bakery tab above, select a dessert to your desire, press order, and enjoy!
